Just click on a line to diplay the pane or view its children.

You can use the keyboard's arrow to navigate in the tree.

To enable the keyboard navigation, just click on a line.

  • Use ↑ and ↓ to go to the previous or the next line
  • Use → to display pane or view children of the current line
  • Use ← to go back to the previous column.

You can type a letter to select the line which begin with this letter.

You can click on the path to navigate in columns.
